About This Event

Join us for an insightful session on Postman API Testing and API Security, conducted by a Postman Campus Expert. This workshop will be led by Mr. Nisal, a highly respected and professional tech speaker known across the island for his engaging and practical sessions.

If you're a student in IT or Software Engineering, this is a must-attend event! API skills are essential for modern development and will give you a strong edge in internships and job interviews.

Don't miss this opportunity to learn from one of the best!

About the Speaker

Mr. Nisal Gunawardhana

Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador | Postman Student Leader

Founder @apestore.lk | LinkedIn Top Voice

Nisal Gunawardhana is a passionate tech advocate and entrepreneur who has made significant contributions to the student developer community. As a Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador and Postman Student Leader, he helps fellow students navigate the tech landscape and build valuable skills. He is also the founder of apestore.lk and has been recognized as a LinkedIn Top Voice for his insightful content on technology trends and education.
